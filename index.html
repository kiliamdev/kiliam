About

KILIAM (Bence) is a 22-year-old music producer and programmer from Hungary, making waves in both the EDM scene and the world of software development. With a dark, emotional progressive house sound, KILIAM draws inspiration from industry giants like Martin Garrix, Avicii, and Matisse & Sadko. He began his musical journey at just 14 years old, driven by a passion to create music that resonates with listeners worldwide.

In addition to his music production, KILIAM is an avid programmer, developing desktop applications and working on exciting video game projects. His first completed project was a weather request application, allowing users to access weather information for cities and countries around the globe. Alongside this, he also creates websites, merging his love for technology with his artistic vision.

KILIAM's music has already earned support from major artists like Hardwell and Nicky Romero (Protocol Radio 616), with releases on respected labels such as FeelQ Recordings, Euphonix Recordings, Jupita, Stardust, INVIBES, and Backlit. With every track, he crafts music that connects with fans on a deeper level, blending emotional melodies with powerful drops.

As his journey unfolds in both music and programming, one thing is certain—this is just the beginning. Stay tuned for more from this rising talent as he continues to push boundaries in both fields.
